Welcome to TUlon! The app helps you plan trips, track expenses, manage your budget, and settle up with travel companions at the end.
🚀

Getting Started

#Action
1Open www.tulon.app in your browser or download from Google Play
2Sign in with Google in one tap — or register with email and password
3Tap "+ New Trip" to create your first trip
4Tap the trip → Budget Management → ⚙️ Trip Settings to add destination, dates and participants
5Add expenses, plan activities in the calendar, share with your group

Trip Settings

⚙️
Trip Settings
The foundation of your trip — fill in before you start
🌍 Destination & Dates
The place you're going and your departure/return dates — the app calculates the number of days automatically
👥 Participants
Important! Add the names of all families or people traveling — this is the basis for splitting expenses
💱 Input Currency
The default currency shown in expense forms
💲 Display Currency
Choose ₪ / $ / € — all totals and the settlement will be shown in this currency at live rates
💰 Planned Budget
Enter an amount — a color-coded gauge shows how much is left
💳

Expenses

💳
Logging Expenses
Every expense during the trip
📂 Categories
Flight ✈️ · Hotel 🏨 · Attractions 🎡 · Food 🍜 · Taxi 🚕 · Other 📦
💱 Multi-currency
Enter any amount in any currency — converted to your default currency automatically at live rates
📷 Receipt scanning
Tap the camera icon and photograph a receipt — AI fills in the amount, currency and description
✈️ Flight Expense
Flight number & times
Displayed on the flight card and in the calendar — also used for departure reminders
🏨 Hotel Expense
Check-in / Check-out
The hotel appears on every night of your stay in the calendar
💸 Splitting Expenses
👥 Shared / 👤 Personal
Shared — included in the group settlement. Personal — not counted
Who was there?
Everyone is checked by default. Tap anyone who wasn't there to remove them from the split
💳 Who paid?
Tap "+ Add payer" → choose a person → enter the amount. Multiple payers per expense are supported
Example: 3 families went on a tour costing ₪300. Only Smith and Jones attended. Paid by: Smith ₪300
→ At the end: Jones owes Smith ₪150
📍 Address
Add an address — tap to open Google Maps, and the place appears on the Map screen
✅ Mark as paid
Locks the exchange rate — the converted amount won't change if the rate shifts

Budget & Settlement

💰
Budget & Settlement
Expense summary and who owes whom
📊 Budget gauge
🟢 up to 70% · 🟡 70–90% · 🔴 over 90%
🥧 Category chart
Pie chart with a breakdown of expenses by category
💸 Who owes whom

Sharing a Trip

🔗
Invite by Link
The easiest way to share with your group
How to create
Open trip → tap 👥 → scroll down → tap "🔗 Generate Invite Link"
Send
📲 Send directly via WhatsApp, or 📋 copy the link
What happens
They tap the link → sign in or register → the trip appears in their account automatically
Revoke link
Tap 🗑️ — anyone already joined stays, but no new people can join
📧
Share with a Specific Email
For precise sharing with individual permissions
How to share
Open trip → tap 👥 → enter email → tap Share
Change permissions
Tap the button next to their name to switch between Edit and View only
Remove
Tap ✕ next to the person's name
💸

Settlement at the End of the Trip

#What to do
1In Trip Settings — add the names of all families / people traveling
2For each shared expense — mark who was there
3Tap "+ Add payer" → enter who paid and how much
4At the end — open Budget and scroll down to see who owes whom
Example — 3 families:
🍽️ Restaurant ₪300 — only Smith & Jones. Paid by: Smith ₪300
🚌 Tour ₪600 — all three. Paid by: Jones ₪400, Green ₪200
🏨 Hotel ₪900 — all three. Paid by: Smith ₪900

Result: Green owes Smith ₪100 · Jones owes Smith ₪50
