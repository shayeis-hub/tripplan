User Guide
Version 6.0
Welcome to TUlon! The app helps you plan trips, track expenses, manage your budget, and settle up with travel companions at the end. This guide explains everything you need to know — in plain, simple language.
🎉 What's New in Version 6.0
  • Smart Expense Splitting 💸 – For each shared expense you can mark exactly who was there and who paid how much. At the end of the trip the app calculates who owes whom
  • View-Only Sharing 👁️ – Share the trip with people who can see the itinerary and calendar but not expenses or budget
  • Manage Shared Members 👥 – Change permissions or remove participants from the share window at any time
  • Activity Type in Calendar 🥾 – When adding a daily activity choose whether it's a hike, restaurant, transport, or general – shown as an icon on the timeline
  • Start & End Time for Activities 🕐 – Set when an activity starts and ends – it appears in the right slot on the hourly timeline
  • Activities Saved to Cloud ☁️ – Activities added in the calendar are saved automatically and never disappear
→

Getting Started

# Action
1 Open www.tulon.co.il in your mobile browser, on your computer, or download from Google Play
2 Tap "New user? Sign up" and enter your email and password
3 Tap "+ New Trip" to create your first trip
4 In the Destination screen: add the names of all families / people traveling (important for expense splitting)
5 Start adding expenses, activities, and flight and hotel details
6 Share the trip with your group via the 👥 Share button inside the trip

Settlement at the End of the Trip

TUlon automatically calculates who owes money to whom – so after the trip everyone knows exactly what to pay.
# What to do
1 In the Destination screen – add the names of all families / people traveling
2 For each shared expense – mark who was there (tap anyone who wasn't to remove them)
3 Tap "+ Add payer" and enter who paid and how much
4 At the end of the trip – open the Budget screen and scroll down to see who owes whom
Full example – 3 families on a trip:

🍽️ Restaurant ₪300 – only Cohen and Levi went. Paid: Cohen ₪300
🚌 Tour ₪600 – all three. Paid: Levi ₪400, Israeli ₪200
🏨 Hotel ₪900 – all three. Paid: Cohen ₪900

Automatic calculation:
→ Israeli owes Cohen ₪100
→ Levi owes Cohen ₪50
