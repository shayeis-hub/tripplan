User Guide
Version 7.0
Welcome to Tulon! The app helps you plan trips, track expenses, manage your budget, and settle up with travel companions at the end. This guide explains everything you need to know — in plain language.
🎉 What's New in Version 7.0
  • New structure – Budget & Trip separated 🗂️ – When you open a trip you'll see two big buttons: "Budget Management" (expenses & budget) and "Trip Management" (calendar, recommendations & more). Much easier to navigate
  • Packing List 🎒 – A new screen to manage your packing checklist, with categories and a progress bar
  • Map Screen 🗺️ – See all the places from your expenses on a map in one tap
  • Tap an activity in the calendar 📅 – Tap any purple activity in day view to see full details and an edit button
  • Redesigned Recommendations ✨ – Agoda full-width at the top for hotels, Viator & GetYourGuide side by side for activities, AI recommendations below
  • Invite by link 🔗 – Send an invite link via WhatsApp — no need to enter anyone's email. Anyone with the link can join the trip
→

Getting Started

#Action
1Open www.tulon.co.il on your phone, computer, or download from Google Play
2Tap "New user? Sign up" and enter your email and password
3Tap "+ New Trip" to create your first trip
4Tap the trip → choose Budget Management → tap ⚙️ Trip Settings to add destination, dates and participants
5Start adding expenses, planning activities in the calendar, and preparing your packing list
6Share the trip via the 👥 button — with a specific email or an open link for the whole group

App Structure

When you open a trip you'll see a home screen with two large buttons. Tap what you need:
💳 Budget Management
  • ⚙️ Trip Settings (destination, dates, people)
  • 💳 Expenses
  • 💰 Budget & Settlement
🌴 Trip Management
  • 📅 Calendar
  • ✨ Recommendations
  • 🎒 Packing List
  • 🗺️ Map
💡 The 🏠 button in the header always returns to the trip home screen. The ← button goes back to the full trip list

Budget Section

⚙️
Trip Settings
Trip details, participants and planned budget
🌍 Destination
The place you're traveling to — e.g. Thailand, Paris, New York
📅 Dates
Departure and return dates — the app calculates the number of days automatically
💱 Currency
Choose the main currency. Add additional currencies — converted automatically at live rates
👥 Participants
Important! Add the names of all families or people traveling — this is the basis for expense splitting
💰 Planned Budget
Enter a budget amount — you'll see a color-coded gauge in the Budget screen showing how much is left
💡 To access settings — tap ⚙️ Trip Settings at the top of the Expenses screen
💳
Expenses
Log every expense during the trip
📂 Categories
Flight ✈️, Hotel 🏨, Attractions 🎡, Food 🍜, Taxi 🚕, Other 📦
💱 Multi-currency
Enter amounts in any currency — converted to your base currency at live rates
📷 Receipt scanning
Tap the camera icon, photograph a receipt — AI fills in the amount, currency and description automatically
✈️ Flight Expense
✈️ Flight number
E.g. LY316 — displayed on the flight card and in the calendar
🛫 Departure / Landing
Displayed on the flight card and used for reminders
🔔 Reminder
Choose 2–8 hours before departure to get a reminder to head to the airport
🏨 Hotel Expense
Check-in / Check-out
Enter dates — the hotel appears on every night of your stay in the calendar
💸 Splitting Expenses
👥 Shared / 👤 Personal
Shared = included in group settlement. Personal = yours only, not counted for others
Who was there?
All participants are checked by default. Tap anyone who wasn't there to remove them from the split
💳 Who paid?
Tap "+ Add payer", choose a person and enter how much they paid. Multiple payers per expense are supported
Example: 3 families went on a tour costing ₪300. Only Smith and Jones attended. Paid by: Smith ₪300
→ At the end: Jones owes Smith ₪150
✅ Mark as paid
Locks the exchange rate — the amount won't change if the rate shifts
📍 Address
Add an address — tap to open Google Maps, and it will also appear on the Map screen
💰
Budget & Settlement
Expense summary and who owes whom
📊 Budget gauge
How much of the budget has been spent: 🟢 up to 70%, 🟡 70–90%, 🔴 over 90%
🥧 Category chart
Pie chart with breakdown — tap any slice for details
💸 Who owes whom
Fully automatic calculation — see exactly who needs to pay whom and how much
📤 Export report
Tap 📄 in the header for a formatted report with all expenses and the settlement
💡 Only "Shared" expenses are included in the settlement calculation. "Personal" expenses are excluded

Sharing a Trip

🔗 Invite by Link — The Easiest Way
New! Send an invite link to the whole group via WhatsApp — anyone with the link can join. No need to enter emails one by one.
TopicDetails
How to create a linkOpen trip → tap 👥 → scroll down → tap "🔗 Generate Invite Link"
SendingTap 📲 to send directly via WhatsApp, or 📋 to copy the link
What happens to the inviteeThey tap the link → sign in or register → the trip appears automatically in their account
View onlyYou can set it so invitees only see the calendar and recommendations — not expenses or budget
Revoke linkTap 🗑️ to revoke — anyone already joined stays, but no new people can join
📧 Share with a Specific Email
TopicDetails
How to shareOpen trip → tap 👥 → enter email → tap Share
What they seeThe entire trip — expenses, budget, calendar — and they can edit
Real-time syncEvery change syncs instantly to all participants
Change permissionsTap the button next to their name to switch between Edit and View Only
Remove participantTap ✕ next to their name to remove them from the trip
✨ Share as Inspiration
TopicDetails
What is it?Send a read-only link — they see what you did and when, without prices
How to useOpen trip → tap ✨ → uncheck what to hide → tap "Create link"
What they can doView and import the trip to their own account — without any prices
Account required?To view — no. To import — yes

Settlement at the End of the Trip

Tulon automatically calculates who owes money to whom — so after the trip everyone knows exactly what to pay.
#What to do
1In Trip Settings — add the names of all families / people traveling
2For each shared expense — mark who was there (tap anyone who wasn't to remove them)
3Tap "+ Add payer" and enter who paid and how much
4At the end of the trip — open Budget and scroll down to see who owes whom
Full example — 3 families:

🍽️ Restaurant ₪300 — only Smith & Jones. Paid by: Smith ₪300
🚌 Tour ₪600 — all three. Paid by: Jones ₪400, Green ₪200
🏨 Hotel ₪900 — all three. Paid by: Smith ₪900

Automatic calculation:
→ Green owes Smith ₪100
→ Jones owes Smith ₪50
