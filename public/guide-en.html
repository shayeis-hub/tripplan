Welcome to TUlon! The app helps you plan trips, track expenses, manage your budget, and coordinate with travel companions. This guide explains every screen and feature clearly and concisely.

Getting Started

# Action
1 Open www.tulon.co.il in your mobile browser, on your computer, or download from Google Play
2 Tap "New user? Sign up" and enter your email and password
3 Tap the bell icon 🔔 in the top bar and allow notifications to receive reminders
4 Tap "+ New Trip" to create your first trip
5 Share your trip with friends via the 👥 Share button inside the trip
6 Tap ❓ at any time to open this guide from within the app
📱

The 5 Main Screens

🌴
Screen 1 – Destination & Trip Settings
The first screen for every trip – basic details
🌍 Destination
The name of the place you're traveling to (e.g. Thailand, Paris, New York)
📅 Dates
Departure and return dates – the app calculates the number of days automatically
💱 Default Currency
The main currency for the trip; tap "Add currency" to add more currencies with live exchange rates
👥 Participants
Add the names of all travel companions – used for expense splitting and settlement
💰 Planned Budget
Enter a total budget in ILS – you'll get a progress bar in the Budget screen with an overage alert
💡 Tip: All details can be edited at any point during the trip
💳
Screen 2 – Expenses
Track every expense during your trip
📂 Categories
Flight ✈️, Hotel 🏨, Attractions 🎡, Food 🍜, Taxi 🚕, Other 📦
💱 Multi-currency
Enter amounts in any currency – automatically converted to ILS at the live exchange rate
📷 Receipt scanning
newTap the camera icon, photograph your receipt – AI automatically fills in the amount, currency, and description
✈️ Flights only
✈️ Flight number
newEnter the flight number (e.g. LY316) – displayed on the expense card and timeline
🛫 Departure time
Enter the departure time to get an automatic reminder before the flight
🛬 Landing time
newEnter the estimated landing time – displayed alongside the departure time on the flight card
🔔 Reminder time
Choose how many hours before the flight to receive a reminder: 2 / 3 / 4 / 5 / 6 / 8 hours
🏨 Hotels only
Check-in / Check-out
Enter stay dates – the amount covers the entire stay; the hotel appears on every night of the stay
🎡 Activities & Other
🕐 Start time
newAdd a start time to place the event on the hourly timeline in Day View
🕔 End time
newAdd an end time – the app calculates and displays the activity duration automatically
All categories
📍 Address
Add an address for quick navigation – tapping opens Google Maps directly
👥 / 👤 Shared / Personal
Shared = enters the group settlement; Personal = yours only, not included in settlement
✅ Paid
Mark as "Paid" to lock the exchange rate – the ILS amount won't change if the rate changes later
✏️ Edit
Tap the pencil icon to edit an existing expense, or 🗑️ to delete it
🔍 Search
Search bar for filtering expenses by description, category, or payment status
💡 Tip: Navigate between dates using the top bar with colored dots
💰
Screen 3 – Budget
Full financial summary with charts and settlement
📊 Budget Progress
Color-coded progress bar: 🟢 up to 70%, 🟡 70–90%, 🔴 above 90%; shows amount remaining / overage with a Spent / Paid / Remaining breakdown
🔢 Animated KPIs
newTotal expenses, paid, unpaid, and expense count – with a smooth counting animation when you open the screen
🥧 Pie Chart
Interactive chart – tap a slice to see details; broken down by category
📈 Category Bars
Visual breakdown of expenses by category with percentages
💸 Settlement
Automatic calculation of who owes whom – based only on shared expenses
📤 PDF Export
Tap "📤 Export" for a formatted report with budget, daily breakdown, and final settlement – exported in the current language, ready to print or save as PDF
💱 Currency Converter
Tap 💱 to open a quick conversion calculator for any currency pair
💡 Tip: Settlement only counts "shared" expenses – "personal" expenses are excluded from the calculation
📅
Screen 4 – Calendar
Two views: monthly overview and hourly day timeline
📅 Month View
Monthly grid
Weekly grid with colored dots: teal = flight, green = hotel, yellow = expenses, purple = activities
Month navigation
← → arrows to navigate months (restricted to months within the trip range)
Tap a day
Tap any day to switch to the Day view with the hourly timeline
🕐 Day View
Hourly timeline
05:00–23:00 view – flights, check-in/out, and time-stamped expenses placed precisely on the timeline
🕐 Activity duration
newActivities with a start and end time show their duration on the timeline (e.g. "09:00–11:30 · 2.5 hrs")
👆 Swipe between days
newSwipe right for the previous day, left for the next – or use the ← → arrows in the header
🏨 Hotel stay days
"Continuing stay at…" shown as a banner at the top of the day instead of on the timeline – doesn't block activities
🌤️ Weather
Daily forecast: weather, min/max temperature, rain probability (available up to 16 days ahead)
🗺️ Navigation
Events with an address show a map icon – tap to open Google Maps
✏️ Activities
Tap "✏️ Activities" on any day to add custom activities with an optional start/end time
💡 Tip: An activity with a time appears on the timeline – without a time it appears in the "No time set" section at the bottom
✨
Screen 5 – Tips all new
AI recommendations + book hotels & activities
🤖 AI Recommendations
newTap "Get Recommendations" – Claude AI analyzes your destination and returns must-see attractions, top restaurants, and one local insider tip
🗺️ Google Maps
new"Open in Maps" button next to each recommendation – opens Google Maps to search the place directly
🏨 Book Hotels
Links to Agoda and Booking.com – open with your destination and trip dates pre-filled
🎡 Book Activities
Links to Viator and GetYourGuide – search tours and activities at your destination
⏳ Skeleton Loader
A smooth animation plays while recommendations load – no blank screen
💡 Recommendations are always in English for best quality – Google Maps opens in your device's GPS language

Sharing Trips

👥 Collaborative Sharing
TopicDetails
How to shareOpen a trip → tap the 👥 Share button → enter your friend's email address
What the friend seesAfter signing in with the same email – the trip appears automatically with a "Shared" badge
Real-time syncEvery change (expense, activity) syncs instantly to all participants
PermissionsAll participants can add and edit expenses
Sending the inviteAfter sharing, tap 📲 to send via WhatsApp or 📋 to copy the link
✨ Share as Inspiration
TopicDetails
What is it?Send a friend a read-only link – they see what you did and when, without prices
How to useOpen a trip → tap the ✨ button → uncheck items to hide → tap "Create link"
PrivacyHide specific items (e.g. hotel price) before creating the link
What the recipient can doView the items, check what interests them, and tap "Import" to add them to their own trip
Account required?To import – yes. To view only – no, the link is open to everyone
SendingTap 📲 to send via WhatsApp or 📋 to copy the link

Push Notifications

Notification typeWhen it arrives
✈️ Airport reminderCustomizable! 2 / 3 / 4 / 5 / 6 / 8 hours before departure time (your choice when entering the flight)
🏨 Check-inOn the morning of check-in day (08:00)
